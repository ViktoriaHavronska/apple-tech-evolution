MacBook Models

MacBook (2006)

The first MacBook replaced the iBook and used Intel processors. It became Apple's main consumer laptop.

MacBook Air (2008)

Introduced as the world's thinnest laptop by Apple. It focused on portability and lightweight design.

MacBook Pro (2006)

Designed for professional users with powerful hardware and advanced graphics capabilities.

MacBook Retina (2015)

Introduced a super thin design and high resolution Retina display.

MacBook M1 (2020)

First MacBook powered by Apple's own Apple Silicon chip. It dramatically improved performance and battery life.
