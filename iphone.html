iPhone Models

Original iPhone (2007)

The first iPhone introduced multitouch controls and a full-screen design. It revolutionized the smartphone industry.

iPhone 3G (2008)

Added support for 3G mobile networks and the App Store.

iPhone 3GS (2009)

Improved performance and introduced video recording.

iPhone 4 (2010)

Introduced the Retina display and a new glass-and-steel design.

iPhone 4S (2011)

First iPhone with Siri voice assistant.

iPhone 5 (2012)

Larger 4-inch display and Lightning connector.

iPhone 5S (2013)

First smartphone with Touch ID fingerprint sensor.

iPhone 6 / 6 Plus (2014)

Introduced larger displays and a new rounded design.

iPhone 7 (2016)

Removed headphone jack and added water resistance.

iPhone X (2017)

Introduced Face ID and an edge-to-edge OLED display.

iPhone 11 (2019)

Dual camera system and improved battery life.

iPhone 12 (2020)

First iPhone with 5G connectivity and flat-edge design.

iPhone 13 (2021)

Improved cameras and longer battery life.

iPhone 14 (2022)

Introduced crash detection and satellite emergency features.

iPhone 15 (2023)

Switched from Lightning to USB-C and added Dynamic Island.
